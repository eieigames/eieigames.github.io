Sepsis 2.0

Sepsis 2.0 is a tactical mobile strategy game in development. The player controls a microbial population inside living tissue while immune cells hunt, surround, and destroy the infection.

Planned gameplay

Microbes move across a tile-based battlefield, consume resources from viable tissue, divide, and release toxins. Damaged tissue becomes less useful, so successful colonies must keep moving while using positioning and sacrifice to resist leukocytes.

A strategy game inspired by biology

The design uses recognisable ideas from infection and immune defence, but turns them into clear tactical rules rather than attempting to be a clinical simulation. The aim is a game that feels medically grounded while remaining understandable and fun.

Release status

Sepsis 2.0 is not yet available for download. A Google Play release is planned after development, balancing, and testing are complete.
