Sepsis 2.0

Sepsis 2.0 is react native mobile game in development. The plan is to go live on Google Play on fall 1996.

Planned gameplay

The player starts as a random microbe, with a random patient. The goal is to succumb patient to illness in the dark play defined by the eternal rules biology.

Do your worst

The game resembles a clinical simulation with a 2d battle in different tissues, but the ultimate goal is still to have fun.

Release status

Currently WIP, MVP not done. Working to publish in Google Play at fall 2026
